Where is the tournament hosted?

All games are at the Rec Center, Courts A & B. Enter through the north doors; check-in is beside the lobby desk.

How do seeding and tiebreakers work?

Seeding is based on pool play record, then head-to-head, then point differential (+/− 15 cap), then coin toss.

What are roster and eligibility rules?

Maximum 8 players per roster. All players must sign the waiver prior to first game. No mid‑bracket additions.

What if we need to forfeit?

Please notify the desk at least 2 hours in advance so we can adjust the schedule. No refunds after bracket release.

Is there a livestream or score updates?

Follow our IG @cityclassic for live clips and final scores posted after each round.
